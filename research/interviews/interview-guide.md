# Learner and expert interview guide

## Learner interview

1. Walk me through what you studied yesterday.
2. How did you decide what to study?
3. Show the apps, PDFs, channels, or notes you used.
4. What happened after the last wrong answer?
5. How do you remember weak areas?
6. When did you last leave a mock unfinished, and why?
7. Which explanation source do you trust most?
8. Show one Tamil/English explanation that was hard to understand.
9. When two sources disagree, what do you do?
10. What have you paid for during preparation?
11. Complete the prototype tasks without coaching.
12. Would you use this for seven days and allow usage measurement?

Avoid leading questions such as “Would an AI tutor help?”

## Expert/reviewer interview

- What defects make a question unusable?
- Which official sources resolve disputes?
- Which Tamil terms require a controlled glossary?
- Which subjects or item types are highest risk?
- How long does answer and explanation review take?
- What can be automated safely?
- What reviewer qualifications and escalation are needed?

## Commitment signals

Record pilot signup, actual seven-day participation, referral/community introduction, shared study routine, expert review time, or payment/early-access commitment.
